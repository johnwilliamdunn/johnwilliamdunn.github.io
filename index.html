I resolve to keep showing up and trying to ask for help when I need it which is hard for me to do. I am hoping I can get more practice with CSS and PHP as I don't know either at this time.

Week 2

The second week has been harder than the first. I'm grappling with the fact that my ability to get paid to do this type of work is limited by my experience and how I will make ends meet as I gain experience. I am letting the fear of not making money interfere with my focus on the subject we are learning I'm afraid, but I did hire a tutor this week to ensure I don't fall behind. I need to focus on my coding skills and continue to build them and use this time to my advantage. Reflecting on my identity, I don't have an online presence currently and that is something I need to address. I have been thinking of how to build my identity online and branding but I don't have a plan so far.

Week 3

The third week we played a game as a team. I personally am happy to go along with the group and don't feel the need to control the interactions. I asked my team for suggestions before making moves to ensure I was making the best decision for the group. On a personal level I hit the wall by Thursday and needed to get away from the work. I had to take a break because I was already feeling burnt out with all of the lecture time.

Week 4

The fourth week has gone better, I am starting to feel more comfortable and starting to understand what the code does that I'm writing. We have as a capstone group gone from forming and storming to performing which is a good turn of events. I am getting the test classes written now and attempting the bootstrap challenge which is easier I think than pure CSS but still a new learning experience.
